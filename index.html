🎯 OBJECTIVE

Eliminate all rival agents before the 5-minute timer expires. Each rival you eliminate grants +1 Point. The agent with the most points when time runs out wins the match.

⚡ TRION SYSTEM

Every trigger action consumes Trion (energy). Attacks, shields, and support triggers all cost Trion. When Trion reaches 0, you can no longer use triggers — but you stay in the fight! BAIL OUT only triggers when HP reaches 0.

💡 Trion does NOT recharge! Conserve energy or fight with your fists.

❤️ HP & DAMAGE

Each agent has HP (Hit Points). When HP reaches 0, the agent is eliminated. Different triggers deal varying damage based on the attacker's Attack stat and the weapon's base power.

💼 BRIEFCASE SETUP

Before battle, configure your Trigger Briefcase with 4 Main and 4 Sub trigger slots. Click any slot in the lobby to swap triggers. Each agent has a default loadout you can customize.

🏆 MATCH MODES

SOLO: Free-for-all battle against AI rivals. Select which agents to deploy and set difficulty.

SQUAD: Tactical 3v3v3 Team Rank Wars. Pick your teammates and face two rival squads. Points are tallied per team.

🛑 BAIL OUT

When eliminated, you can still spectate the match using WASD to scroll the camera. Wait for the match to end naturally or click REDEPLOY to start a new match.

⚔️ ATTACKER (Melee)

Kogetsu — Standard blade. High damage per hit, moderate reach.
Scorpion — Lightweight agile blade. Ultra fast attacks, short range. Versatile close-quarters weapon.
Raygust — Heavy defense-oriented blade. Slower attacks but durable. Pairs with Thruster for dash combos.
Senku (Kogetsu) — Extended range slash. Sends a blade wave at medium range.
Mole Claw — Underground surprise attack. Launches blade from beneath the ground.

🔫 SHOOTER (Ranged)

Asteroid — Straight-line bullet. High power, no homing.
Hound — Homing bullet. Tracks enemies but deals less damage.
Viper — Custom-path bullet. Draw a flight path for curved shots.
Meteora — Explosive shell. Detonates on impact, area damage.

🔫 GUNNER (Ranged Weapons)

Assault Rifle — Rapid-fire automatic. High rate, moderate damage.
Shotgun — Close-range spread. Devastating at close distance.

🎯 SNIPER (Long Range)

Egret — Standard sniper. Good range and damage balance.
Lightning — Fastest sniper. Low damage, ultra-fast projectile.
Ibis — Heavy sniper. Extreme damage, can destroy walls.

🛡️ DEFENSE & SUPPORT

Shield — Trion barrier. Blocks incoming projectiles. Equip both Main & Sub for Full Shield (360°).
Bagworm — Stealth cloak. Hides you from enemy radar.
Grasshopper — Launch pad. Creates a bounce platform that boosts you in your movement direction.
Spider — Tripwire net. Anchors between walls to trap/slow enemies.
Thruster — Raygust booster. Dash forward at high speed.
Lead Bullet — Heavy weight infusion. Slows hit enemies dramatically.

💫 COMPOSITE BULLETS (Spacebar Fusion)

Gimlet (Asteroid + Asteroid) — Armor-piercing bullet. Breaks shields.
Tomahawk (Hound + Meteora) — Homing missile. Tracks and explodes.
Salamander (Hound + Viper) — Intelligent tracker. Complex curves with homing.
Hornet (Asteroid + Hound) — Hybrid bullet. Semi-homing with high damage.

💡 Equip Shooter triggers in BOTH Main & Sub slots to fuse. 1.8s charge time, can't move during fusion!
